Corey Qureshi (they/them) is a writer, musician, and parent based in Philadelphia. Through poetry, fiction and nonfiction, their work focuses on the exploitative conditions of labor under capitalism, interpersonal relationships and identity. They work at an LGBTQ+ center. They're a Taurus that loves Scifi, Nintendo, sunlight, clean spaces, and eating.
